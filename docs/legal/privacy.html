Usage Information:

  • App features accessed

  • Time spent on screens

  • Bot interactions
  • Search queries
  • In-app purchase history

Location Information:

  • Country and region (derived from IP address)

We may receive information about you from:

  • Social Media Platforms: If you choose to sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture (subject to your privacy settings on those platforms)
